The Impact of Advanced Technology Enhancing Human Activity Detection Through AI: A Comparison of Modern ML Algorithms

Overview:-This research focuses on how advanced technology and artificial intelligence (AI) can improve human activity detection (HAD). By comparing different modern machine learning (ML) algorithms, the study evaluates their effectiveness, accuracy, and potential applications in real-world scenarios such as healthcare monitoring, smart environments, and human-computer interaction.

Objectives:- To analyze the role of AI in enhancing human activity detection, To compare the performance of modern ML algorithms, To explore practical applications in various domains

Methodology:- In this study, the methodology involved several key steps to ensure accurate evaluation of human activity detection through AI. The process began with data preprocessing and feature extraction, where raw sensor data was cleaned, normalized, and transformed into meaningful features suitable for machine learning models. Next, multiple machine learning algorithms, including Random Forest, Support Vector Machine (SVM), and Neural Networks, were implemented to analyze their comparative performance. These models were trained and tested on standardized datasets to ensure consistency and reliability of results. Finally, the performance of each algorithm was evaluated using widely accepted metrics such as accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score, providing a comprehensive understanding of their strengths and limitations in detecting human activities.

Key Findings:- AI-driven ML algorithms significantly improve activity recognition accuracy, Deep learning models outperform traditional ML methods in complex scenarios, Potential applications in healthcare, IoT devices, and smart systems are highly promising.
